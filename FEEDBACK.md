## General First Impressions
**Date:** March 14, 2026
**Testing Duration:** Initial testing
**Device:** OnePlus

### What works well?
- Instagram-style swiping feels engaging
- Dark mode toggle works great
- Progress indicator is helpful
- One card at a time keeps focus

### What feels off or confusing?
- ✅ FIXED: Header overlapped with phone status bar
- ✅ FIXED: Content cards were too long and got cut off
- ⚠️ NEEDS WORK: Content level feels too basic/introductory - needs more depth and maturity

**Content Maturity Feedback:**
- Current CRISPR topic reads like a general introduction
- Target audience should be educated adults who want to go deeper
- Should assume some baseline knowledge
- Include more nuanced insights, recent developments, technical details
- Less "what is X" and more "interesting aspects of X you might not know"
- Think: The Economist, Atlantic, or Wired depth, not Wikipedia intro

### Overall rating (1-5): 4 (improved significantly with swipe UI)

### Next Steps:
1. Create content curation skill/guidelines
2. Regenerate topics with higher maturity level
3. Establish consistent voice and depth standards
